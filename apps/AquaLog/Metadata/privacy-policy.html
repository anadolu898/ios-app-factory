Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

AquaLog ("the App") is a hydration tracking application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Hydration logs (drink amounts, beverage types, timestamps)
  • Profile information you provide during onboarding (weight, age, gender, activity level)
  • Your daily hydration goal and preferences
  • Reminder schedule settings

Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you grant permission, AquaLog can read and write water intake data to Apple Health. This data stays on your device and is governed by Apple's privacy policies. We never access your Health data without your explicit consent.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location (WeatherKit queries are processed by Apple, not us)
  • We do not use analytics SDKs that track user behavior
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party

Subscriptions

AquaLog Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a pseudonymous app user ID — never your personal information. See RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Crash Reporting

We may use Apple's built-in crash reporting to improve app stability. Crash reports do not contain personal data.

Data Storage

All your hydration data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers or cloud databases. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

AquaLog is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
